Activities

Our major activities for the upcoming year

Colloquial Marathi Classes

We give you the opportunity to learn basic Marathi from scratch. If you know some basics, you can still understand advanced language skills. This will help you to communicate with locals.

Exploring Pune

We plan to arrange trips around the city to see local heritage sites, museums and enjoy lical Maharashtrian cuisines.

Trekking

Pune treks are bestowed with natures blessings, especially in places like Lohagad trek, Tikona fort, Sinhagarh trek, Pawna lake and Korigad Trek.

Meet for Marathi

These are specifically meant for but not limited to people who know the Marathi language. We will be exploring Marathi literature, culture, the language here. This will include poetry recitals, book discussions, word puzzles, and many more such activities.

Omkar Utsav

Ganesh festival is the most attractive event in Pune and it's also celebrated in IISER. Many departments like decoration, feast, logistic work together to make it a success in eco friendly way.
